# Privacy Policy for SpeakDaily

**Last Updated: January 29, 2026**

## Introduction

SpeakDaily ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SpeakDaily (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

- **Language Preferences**: Your selected interface language and translation language
- **Learning Progress**: Completed scenarios, phrases learned, and achievement data
- **Settings**: App theme preferences (dark/light mode), audio settings, and customization choices

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in the app, scenarios accessed
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identifiable Information**: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address
- **Location Data**: We do not track your precise location
- **Contacts or Photos**: We do not access your device contacts or photo library
- **Microphone**: We do not record or transmit audio from your device

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 To Provide and Maintain the Service
- Deliver personalized learning experiences
- Track your learning progress and achievements
- Remember your preferences and settings

### 2.2 To Improve the App
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Fix bugs and improve app performance
- Develop new features based on user behavior

### 2.3 To Display Advertisements
- Show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Measure ad performance and engagement

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- **Your learning progress** is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- **App preferences** are stored locally using SharedPreferences
- **No cloud backup**: Your data is not automatically backed up to our servers

### 3.2 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Data encryption at rest
- Secure coding practices
- Regular security updates

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Local data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- You can reset your progress at any time through the app settings

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads.

**Information AdMob may collect:**
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- App usage information
- Ad interaction data

**AdMob Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Opt-out of personalized ads:**
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads

### 4.2 Flutter Text-to-Speech (TTS)

We use the device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to provide audio pronunciation. This service:
- Processes text locally on your device
- Does not transmit data to external servers
- Uses your device's native TTS capabilities
